Understanding Islamic Financing

Mudarabah

A partnership where one party provides capital and the other provides expertise.

Musharakah

A joint venture where all partners contribute capital and share profits and losses.

Ijara

A leasing agreement where the lessor buys and leases an asset to the lessee.
