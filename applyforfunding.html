Your Journey to Riba-Free Growth

1

Submit Your Application

Register your business (KVK), share your pitch, funding need, and team. Choose between Mudarabah (profit-sharing) or Musharakah (shared ownership). Upload your financials, impact analysis, and risk profile.

2

Ethical Screening

We screen projects to ensure Shariah compliance—no riba, no unethical sectors, and a clear social or community benefit.

3

Launch Your Project

Your project is published with a detailed profile, video, funding target, risk insights, and potential returns.

4

Funding Phase

Investors can pledge or invest directly. Track progress and engage with your funders in real time.

5

Payout & Mentorship

Funds are released via escrow in phases. Access mentoring and support from our entrepreneur community.

6

Transparency & Reporting

Provide monthly updates on revenue, milestones, and roadblocks. Build lasting investor trust.

7

Profit Sharing or Exit

Distribute profits based on your agreement. Plan for reinvestment or a smooth exit if scaling or selling.
