Nourish & Grow – Urban Organic Farm

About the Project

Nourish & Grow is an urban agriculture project focused on delivering fresh, pesticide-free produce to underserved communities. With riba-free financing, the project was launched in May 2024 with the aim to build food security and create jobs in the city.

Economic & Social Impact

  • Created 12 part-time jobs for youth and seniors
  • 25 families now receive weekly fresh produce
  • Workshops hosted for school children
  • Partnership with local mosques for sadaqah-based food distribution

Project Updates

Month 1: Greenhouse construction completed, initial crops planted (spinach, tomatoes, mint).
Month 3: First harvest delivered to 12 families. Local volunteers joined the effort.
Month 6: Expanded delivery route, added 5 more families, planning new vertical garden.
