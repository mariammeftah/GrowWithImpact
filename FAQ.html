Frequently Asked Questions

For Investors

How do I invest in a project?

You can register as an investor and browse listed projects. Once verified, you can pledge funds directly through the platform.

Is there a minimum investment amount?

Yes, the minimum amount is €100 to ensure accessibility and diversification.

For Entrepreneurs

How do I submit a project?

Click 'Apply for Funding', fill out your project profile, and submit for review by our Shariah board and platform team.

Are there any fees involved?

Yes, there's a small listing fee and a success-based fee upon funding. These will be transparently shown before publishing.

Shariah Compliance

Is Fairvest certified?

Yes, all projects go through a screening by our certified Shariah advisors.

Which contracts do you use?

We use Mudarabah (profit-sharing) and Musharakah (joint venture) contracts approved by scholars.

Returns

What returns can I expect?

Returns vary per project and are not guaranteed. Estimates are based on the business model and agreed-upon terms.

When do I get paid?

Profit distributions occur according to the agreed schedule—quarterly, bi-annually, or annually.

Risks & Exit

Can I exit my investment early?

Early exit depends on the contract terms. Some projects allow resale or partial exit, others do not.

What happens if a project fails?

As in any business, there is risk. Investors may lose capital, but we screen projects thoroughly to reduce this risk.
